CareerLens AI
SUMMARY
CareerLens AI is an AI-powered career intelligence platform that helps students understand their placement readiness,
discover suitable job roles, identify skill gaps, track career progress, and receive personalized learning roadmaps.

PROBLEM STATEMENT
Students often face the following challenges: Unsure which companies they qualify for; Unclear career direction; Lack
of awareness about required skills; No centralized placement preparation dashboard; Difficulty understanding job
descriptions; Lack of personalized career guidance; No measurable placement readiness score. Current solutions are
fragmented across LinkedIn, Job portals, Placement websites, Resume tools, Course platforms. CareerLens
consolidates everything into a single AI-powered ecosystem.

CORE MODULES
1) Authentication: Sign Up, Login, Forgot Password, Email Verification, Google Login, Logout. 2) User Profile:
personal, professional, academic details, projects, career preferences. 3) Career Dashboard: readiness score, eligible
companies, matched roles, skills, resume score, applications; trends and progress graphs. 4) AI Role Matching:
suggested roles with match %, required/missing skills, salary range, learning path. 5) Company Eligibility Engine:
eligibility by CGPA, skills, projects, certifications, experience for companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Infosys,
TCS, Wipro, Accenture, Cognizant, Capgemini, Deloitte, EY, PwC. 6) Skill Gap Analysis. 7) AI Roadmap Generator
(30/60/90/180-day). 8) Resume Analyzer (ATS, keywords, strength, missing sections, suggestions, grammar). 9) Job
Description Analyzer. 10) AI Career Coach. 11) Project Recommendation Engine. 12) Application Tracker. 13)
Interview Preparation Hub. 14) Learning Resource Center.

DATABASE DESIGN
Tables: USERS, SKILLS, PROJECTS, CERTIFICATIONS, CAREER_ROLES, COMPANIES, ROADMAPS,
