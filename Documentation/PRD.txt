# 1. Executive Summary

CareerLens is an AI-powered web application designed to bridge the gap between education and employability by helping students discover suitable career paths, identify skill gaps, receive personalized learning roadmaps, and prepare for placements through project recommendations, resume building, and interview preparation.

Unlike conventional career guidance platforms that only recommend careers based on questionnaires, CareerLens functions as a complete career development ecosystem. It continuously tracks user progress, adapts recommendations, and provides actionable steps toward becoming industry-ready.

The platform aims to simplify career planning through intelligent recommendations while empowering users with curated learning resources, practical projects, and measurable progress tracking.

---

# 2. Vision Statement

To become the most trusted AI-powered career development platform that enables every learner to make informed career decisions, acquire industry-relevant skills, and confidently transition into their dream profession.

---

# 3. Mission Statement

CareerLens empowers students and professionals with personalized AI-driven career guidance, skill development plans, and practical learning experiences that reduce confusion and accelerate career growth.

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Many students struggle with career planning because they:

• Lack awareness of available career opportunities.
• Do not understand which skills employers require.
• Learn random technologies without structured direction.
• Follow generic online roadmaps.
• Receive conflicting advice from multiple sources.
• Have difficulty selecting projects relevant to their goals.
• Build resumes without understanding ATS optimization.
• Enter placements without sufficient interview preparation.

Current career platforms often focus on only one aspect, such as aptitude tests, online courses, or resume creation, requiring users to switch between multiple services.

---

# 5. Proposed Solution

CareerLens integrates career discovery, AI recommendations, learning roadmaps, project suggestions, resume generation, interview preparation, and progress tracking into a single platform.

The application analyzes a user's interests, strengths, goals, and current skills to recommend suitable careers and provide personalized action plans.

# 12. Success Criteria

The product will be considered successful if:

• Users complete the career assessment.
• Users follow recommended roadmaps.
• Learning completion rates increase.
• Resume downloads increase.
• Daily active users grow consistently.
• User retention improves month over month.
• Users report increased confidence in career decisions.
• Students successfully secure internships and placements.
